# Student User Guide

Welcome to the CSDAC Internship Portal! This portal is your central hub for managing your internship, tracking attendance, and submitting project work.

## Getting Started
Once you pass the initial screening, the administration team will generate your workspace and email you a set of credentials.
1. Navigate to `/login`.
2. Enter your email and the provided password.
3. You will land on your **Student Dashboard**.

## 1. Dashboard
The dashboard summarizes your:
- Attendance percentage
- Enrolled internship and batch details
- Total weeks of progress submitted
- A quick link to download your certificate (if completed)

## 2. Daily Attendance
Navigate to the **Attendance** tab on the sidebar.
- You must click **Check In** every working day.
- The system logs your check-in time using Indian Standard Time (IST).
- You can only check in once per day.
- **Note**: A minimum of 75% attendance is strictly required to be eligible for the final certificate.

## 3. Weekly Progress & GitHub
Navigate to the **Progress** tab.
- At the end of every week, you must submit a summary of what you learned or developed.
- If your work involves coding, provide a direct link to your GitHub commit or Pull Request.
- Mentors will review this submission and leave remarks.

## 4. Project Details
Navigate to the **Project** tab.
- Here you can view the details of the project assigned to you.
- You can update your Live URL (if you have deployed your project) and Repository URL for final evaluation.

## 5. Support & Help Desk
Navigate to the **Support** tab.
- If you face any technical issues with the portal or administrative issues regarding your batch, click **New Ticket**.
- Provide a clear subject and detailed message.
- Support staff typically respond within 24-48 hours.

## 6. Certificates
Once your internship concludes and all criteria are met, the admin will generate your certificate.
- Navigate to the **Certificate** tab to view your grade and certificate number.
- Anyone can verify your certificate's authenticity by visiting the public verification page and entering your unique ID.
